I am Demeter, one of the most famous goddesses. I am the god of agriculture, fertility, and harvest. My daughter Persephone was kidnapped by Hades and I am extremely distraught of that. If anybody could capture Hades and get my Persephone back, I will stop changing the seasons all the time.


How I control the seasons is during spring and summer, I get to visit my daughter but when it becomes fall and winter, I feel desolate and sadness. I only get to see my daughter half a year which is during spring and summer and that's why it's so sunny and happy at that time and not in fall and winter. During that time, Persephone has to stay in The Underworld with the nefarious Hades, while I cry in sadness and hide in my home.
