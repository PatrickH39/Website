Poseidon

@TheRealPoseidon

Hey everybody, I am Poseidon aka the god of the sea; protectors of all waters! I have two brothers and they are Hades and Zeus. I have three sisters too who are Demeter, Hestia, and Hera.

I love to use my trident because I can make earthquakes with them when I’m mad. Earthquakes create tidal waves, ocean storms, and most importantly.. tsunamis. Watching a whole city collapse and all the innocent-looking people scream and run in terror is entertaining.

I absolutely hate Odysseus and I’ve held a grudge on him for a prolonged period of time. Did you know, I’m always referred to as King Neptune in SpongeBob SquarePants? Well, that’s because my Roman name is Neptune and my Greek name is Poseidon. I also use horse chariots to transport myself to one place to another.
